Must watch list (Movies/Series) according to Kylor

My top 3 must watch Movies or Series.

The Godfather

Masterpeice of cinema with great storytelling, character development, and powerful themes of power, loyalty, and family.

Pulp Fiction

Story is told out of chronological order, amazing characters, and very strong dialog. All this with a blend of humor and violence makes it a classic!

Game of Thrones

The character building and constant evolving script has always kept to waiting for more!
